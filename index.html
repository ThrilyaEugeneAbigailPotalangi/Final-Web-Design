About Us

Casabaio Paradise in Likupang is a hidden paradise in North Sulawesi. Surrounded by natural beauty, white sandy beaches, and crystal-clear waters,
this place offers an amazing holiday experience for tourists. Travelers can enjoy snorkeling, diving, or simply relaxing on the beach while enjoying the stunning views. With its natural and uncrowded environment,Casabaio Paradise is an ideal place to unwind and appreciate the beauty of Indonesia's nature.
